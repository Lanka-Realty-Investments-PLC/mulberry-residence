Facilities & Finishes

The apartment scheme consists of 03 towers, each with 14 floors, connected via a podium at ground to second floors. There are 03 floors of car parking (Ground, 1st & 2nd), 11 residential floors (3rd -13th), a 7000 Sq.ft roof top area with two swimming pools, two Gymnasiums, kids play area and landscaped garden.

All units are equipped with a master bedroom and attached bathroom, state-of-the-art kitchen, open plan sitting and dining room with balcony, secondary bedroom, visitor’s bathroom and maid’s toilet and drying area.
